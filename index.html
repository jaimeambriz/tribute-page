Lee Jun-Fan better known as Bruce Lee

Here is a what Bruce Lee's life was like.

  • 1940 - November 27 Lee Jun Fan, Bruce Lee is born at 7:12am during the year of the dragon and the hour of the dragon (between 7:00 a.m. and 9:00 a.m.) at the Jackson Street Hospital in San Francisco Chinatown.
  • 1941 Bruce and his parents move back to Kowloon Hong Kong.
  • 1946 While still in Hong Kong Bruce makes his first film called "Beginning of a Boy. "
  • 1952 Bruce begins attendnig La Salle College.
  • 1953 Bruce begins taking Kung-Fu lessons under master Sifu Yip Man aftre getting beaten up in a street fight.
  • 1954 Bruce begains practicing cha-cha dancing.
  • 1958 Bruce wins Crown Colony Cha-Cha Championship.
  • 1959 Bruce moves back to the United States. Arriving in San Fransisco then moving to Seattle he enrolls in Edison Technical School and earns his HS diploma, shortly after he begins training his martial arts in backyards and city parks.
  • 1961 Bruce enrolls at the Univerity of Washington, majoring in Philosophy.
  • 1963 Bruce returns to Hong Kong to visit his family while on Summer vacation. In October he starts the first Jun Fan Kung-Fu Institute.
  • 1964 Bruce marries Linda Emery and opens his second Jun Fan Kung-Fu Institute in Oakland, Ca.
  • 1965
    • February 1 Brendon Lee is born in Oakland, Ca.
    • February 8 Bruce Lee's father passes away in Hong Kong.
    • Bruce is signed to the role on The Green Hornet as sidekick Kato under a 1yr. contract. He also begins to develop the art of JKD (Jeet Kun Do) after having fought Wong Jack Man, another Kung-Fu practiotionar in his area.
  • 1966 Bruce and his family move to Los Angeles, He begins filming The Green Hornet T.V. Series. He also opens his third Jun Fan Kung-Fu Institiute in Los Angeles' Chinatown.
  • 1967-1971
    • Bruce continues to work on various films and T.V.series while offering private lessons for 250/hr. Bruce meets Chuck Norris at the All American Karate Championships in Washington D.C.
    • 1969 April 19 Shannon Lee is born in Santa Moica, Ca.
    • 1971 July Bruce begins filming the "Big Boss " a.k.a. "Fists of Fury " in the United States
  • 1972
    • "The Chineses Conection " is relesed. It grossed more than "Fists of Fury " and established Bruce lee as a superstar. Meanwhile in Italy Bruce was filming "The Return of the Dragon. "
    • December 28 Bruce Lee's brother, James dies of Black Lung in Oakland, CA.
  • 1973
    • February Bruce begins to film "Enter the Dragon "
    • April Filming "Enter the Dragon " is completed. During a studio session for dubing his voice Bruce took a bathroom brake and collapes on the floor, he was found 20 min. later and rushed to the hospital.
    • July 20 Bruce Lee dies at age 33 in Hong Kong of a cerebral edema
    • August 19 "Enter the Dragon " was released
